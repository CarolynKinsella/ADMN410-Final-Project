Store Policies

The Durham House of Pets is always here for you.




Financing
We offer four-month, and twelve-month payment plan options for customers looking to adopt any pet.
Both of our payment plan options are interest fee, so you can enjoy the excitement of bringing home your new best friend, stress free!

Delivery
We deliver any and all products right to your door!
Any address within fifty miles of either of our two regional locations qualify for delivery.

Returns
If you and your pet don't love it, it's on us! Feel free to visit us again to return or exchange any product for another, or for store credit!
*Returns and exchanges valid for up to 30 days after purchase*
